**CellSAM** is a foundation model for cell segmentation that generalizes across cell types, imaging modalities, and experimental conditions. Built on the Segment Anything Model architecture and fine-tuned on a large-scale dataset of over 22 million annotated cells, CellSAM achieves state-of-the-art performance on segmentation benchmarks without per-task fine-tuning.

Published in [Nature Methods (2025)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41592-025-02879-w).
