**SIPEC** is an end-to-end deep-learning pipeline for identification, tracking, pose estimation, and behaviour classification of interacting primates and mice in complex environments. It enables automated analysis of animal behavior from video at scale.

Published in [Nature Machine Intelligence (2022)](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-022-00477-5).
