**MABe22** is a large-scale benchmark for evaluating learned representations of animal behavior across multiple species and tasks. It provides standardized evaluation protocols for self-supervised and supervised methods on behavioral data from mice, flies, and other organisms.

Published at [ICML 2023](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v202/sun23g/sun23g.pdf).
